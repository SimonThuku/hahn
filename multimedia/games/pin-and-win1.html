Pin-and-Win

The competition ended on December 16th, 2012 but you can still play the game for fun!

Just register on www.hahnair.com, enter the "My Hahn" section and click on the menu item "Pin-and-Win Game" to proof your topographical talent and airline knowledge. If you have logged onto your user account you can also click on this link https://ticketing.hahnair.travel/pin-and-win.html

________________________________________________________________

Between November 13th and December 16th, 2012 our Pin-and-Win competition was on. Here are the lucky winners:

Congratulations to  

  • Oleg Snizhko from Ukraine who had the highest score of 1,950,000 points and won a travel voucher worth €1,500
  • Natalia Savytska from Ukraine who scored 1,944,800 points and won a travel voucher worth €1,000
  • Robert Leest from Germany who scored 1,942,000 points and won a travel voucher worth €500

Additionally, we want to congratulate Karina Villacis from Ecuador to an iPad for having invited the most friends, consequently registered on www.hahnair.com, via "Tell a Friend".

Thanks to the spontaneous, 24-hour Pin-and-Win challenges, six agents won shopping vouchers of your choice worth €100

  • Oleg Snizhko from Ukraine won our first 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on November 27th by exactly hitting the 169,000 score.
  • Robert Leest from Germany won our second 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on November 29th by scoring 999,998 points and therewith being the closest to the 999,999 score.
  • Nils Gnadt from Germany won our third 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on December 04th by scoring 250,001 and therewith the closest to the 250,000 score.
  • Sandy Schmidt from Germany won our fourth 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on December 07th by scoring 1,690,010 and therewith the closest to the 1,690,000 score.
  • Steve Hein from Germany won our fifth 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on December 11th by scoring 500,002 and therewith the closest to the 500,000 score.
  • Daniel Schneider from Germany won our sixth 24-hour Pin-and-Win special on December 13th by scoring 879,998 and therewith the closest to the 880,000 score.
